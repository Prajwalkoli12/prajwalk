Road tripping across Georgia

Self-driving my way across Georgia in 14 days with my bestie was surely one of the most fantabulous travel experiences.

With cha cha shots and mindblowing scenery, unique terrains and sprawling landscapes, endless wine and friendly locals – Georgia is surely worth a visit.

Spectacular City Hiking in Hong Kong.

City hikes? What are those? I wondered prior to visiting Hong Kong.

Now imagine a huge metropolis with a bustling skyline and lush green jungles to hike through to catch those views from a vantage point – that’s city hiking for you and it can be experienced best in Hong Kong. They have a hike for every reason.

Traversing across Vietnam by Bus.

Vietnam is best explored by bus. What’s the great thing about these Vietnamese buses is that you can buy open tickets which are perfect for an unplanned traveler such as myself.So, exploring Vietnam on my own, turned out to be a cake walk, thanks to the transport all sorted through these open bus tickets.

You can travel from north to south or visa versa and stop at any of the major cities for as long as you want
