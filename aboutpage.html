ABOUT

Namaste, Guten Tag! I'm Sonal from India, living in Germany and exploring Europe. I've been writing about my travels since 2015. I often travel alone (and sometimes with family of 3). I love European city breaks, nature, adventure, hiking to viewpoints, Yoga, and road trips. I have a think for creating the most amazing travel itineraries and in-depth destination guides which will help you make the most of your trip.

I moved to Germany in 2017 (yay!). So, as a person living in Europe, I present to you my best tips, information and itineraries for traveling in this continent. My way of traveling is not to just visit a place to check it off the list. I try to get my way of getting my best travel experience of that place.

So what’s that like? I like viewpoints, affordable local food, room with a view, good coffee, best photo spots, sunset spots, and just some little things that make my visit extra special. And yes, that’s exactly the kind of stuff I share in my destination guides on my website.
