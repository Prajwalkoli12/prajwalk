DARJEELING

Darjeeling is a town in India's West Bengal state, in the Himalayan foothills.

Once a summer resort for the British Raj elite, it remains the terminus of the narrow-gauge Darjeeling Himalayan Railway, or “Toy Train,” completed in 1881.

It's famed for the distinctive black tea grown on plantations that dot its surrounding slopes. Its backdrop is Mt. Kanchenjunga, among the world’s highest peaks.

ROME

Rome is the capital city of Italy. It is also the capital of the Lazio region, the centre of the Metropolitan City of Rome Capital, and a special comune (municipality) named Comune di Roma Capitale.

With 2,860,009 residents in 1,285 km2 (496.1 sq mi),[2] Rome is the country's most populated comune and the third most populous city in the European Union by population within city limits.

The Metropolitan City of Rome, with a population of 4,355,725 residents, is the most populous metropolitan city in Italy.[3] Its metropolitan area is the third-most populous within Italy.[5] Rome is located in the central-western portion of the Italian Peninsula, within Lazio (Latium), along the shores of the Tiber.


BALI

This island is located in Indonesia and is known for its forested volcanic mountains, beautiful beaches and amazing coral reefs. Try Snorkelling in Sanur beach and have an exciting time.

Like all other adventure places, Bali is also soaked in natural beauty. You can indulge in adventure activities like surfing, canyon tubing and river rafting to experience this beautiful island.

For amateurs, riding an underwater scooter is a unique way to explore the marine world.


PARIS

Paris, France's capital, is a major European city and a global center for art, fashion, gastronomy and culture.

Its 19th-century cityscape is crisscrossed by wide boulevards and the River Seine.

Beyond such landmarks as the Eiffel Tower and the 12th-century, Gothic Notre-Dame cathedral, the city is known for its cafe culture and designer boutiques


LAKSHADWEEP ISLAND

Adoring the south-western coast of India,lies the spectacular coral island chain of Lakshdweep.

These 36 islands,scattered in the Arabian Sea,boast some of the most beautiful beaches in India,crystal-blue waters and stunning coral reefs.

The places to visit in Lakshadweep include the islands of Agatti,Bangaram,Kavaratti,Andretti,Kadmat,Kapeni and Minicony.


NORWAY

Norway is a Scandinivian country encompassing mountains,glaciers and deep coastal fjords.Oslo,the capital,is a city of green spaces and museums.

Norway is also known for fishing,hiking and skiing,notably at Lillehammer's Olympic resort.

About two-thirds of Norway is mountains,and off its much-indented coastline lie,carved by deep glacial fjords,some 50,000 islands.

TOKYO

Tokyo,Japan's busy capital,mixes the ultramodern and the traditional,from neon-lit skyscrapers to historic temples.

The Imperial Palace sits amid large public gardens.The city's many museums offer exhibits ranging from classical art to reconstructed kabuki theater

Different variaties of cherry blossoms bloom at different times,but most hit their peak in Tokyo at the end of March to the beginning of April.
