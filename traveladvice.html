TRAVEL TIPS

Do you find travel planning overwhelming and time consuming? We're here to help. Travel has been our lifestyle for over 22 years now - most of that involved nomadic full tine travel. We made it work as solo travelers, travel as a couple, expat travelers, family travelers, and location independent travelers. We have lived in 5 countries and traveled to over 50, in-depth through Australia, USA, Southeast Asia,UK, and Africa. We aim to share with you information based upon personal experiences NOT desk research or collaborations! This is real, authentic travel to help you do the same.
  • The key ingredients to a smooth family vacation when traveling with kids are to pay attention to their needs, and find the right balance between rest time and activities..

  • Bored and overtired children become cranky and frustrated kids in a hurry.

  • Learn the basics of the local language

  • Manners are universal, use them

  • NEVER complain that the people of the country you are visiting do not speak English

  • Bargain, it is an expected part of a transaction

  • Respect local customs

  • Leave a good impression of your culture on the countries you are visiting

  • Have off the beaten path adventures
